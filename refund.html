Refund Policy


Eligibility for Refunds

At T.R.M.T. Consultancy, we prioritize customer satisfaction and strive to provide high-quality services tailored to your business needs. Refunds are available under the following conditions:


  1. If the service was not delivered as described or failed to meet the agreed-upon objectives.
  2. If a cancellation request is made within 7 days of the initial payment and before any substantial work has commenced.
  3. In cases of service errors or failures on our part that cannot be rectified within a reasonable timeframe.

Timeframe for Refunds

Refund requests must be made within 30 days of the initial service delivery or payment date. Requests made after this period will not be eligible for a refund.


Restocking Fees

Since T.R.M.T. Consultancy provides professional services rather than physical goods, there are no restocking fees. However, a processing fee of 10% of the total service fee may be deducted from the refund to cover administrative costs, except in cases where the refund is due to our error.


Process for Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at [customer service email] with your order number, a detailed description of the issue, and any supporting documentation.
  2. Our team will review your request and may contact you for additional information or clarification.
  3. If your refund request is approved, the refund will be processed within 14 business days, and the funds will be credited to the original method of payment.
