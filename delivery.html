Delivery Policy


Shipping Options Available

T.R.M.T. Consultancy offers digital delivery of all consultancy services and documentation. Clients will receive their deliverables via email or through our secure online portal.


Estimated Delivery Times

The delivery times for our services vary depending on the complexity and scope of the project. General timelines are as follows:

  1. Business Registration Services: 5-10 business days from the date of order confirmation.
  2. Consultancy Services: Delivery times are project-specific and will be outlined in the service agreement.
  3. Customized Documentation: 3-7 business days from the date of order confirmation.

Additional Shipping Charges

There are no additional shipping charges for digital delivery. All service fees include the cost of digital delivery via email or our secure portal.
